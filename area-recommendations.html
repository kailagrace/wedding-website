THINGS TO DO

Wicasset is revered as one of the most beautiful and quaint seaside towns on the East Coast. Five reasons to visit Wiscasset, Maine .

Billy and I love to hike at Oven's Mouth Preserve in Boothebay Harbor. On tripbuzz, it's described as "Oven's Mouth Preserve is located on two peninsulas located adjacent to each other. Visitors to the site find hiking trails from which eagles, osprey, otters and deer are often spotted, an old homestead that can be explored, and a beach." Oven's Mouth Preserve.
